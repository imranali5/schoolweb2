ABOUT US

Welcome to Bes An-Noor Model School

Bes An-Noor Model School is a highly acclaimed educational institution in North Bengal. Founded in 2003 but ‘Bes’ has established its uniqueness within a short period of time. It is located in a pleasant and shady environment.Bes An-Noor's Boys and Girls Division campuses are located at Pransagar, Haripur, Banshihari, Itahar, Malda and so on. Total land area is 18.36 acres and building 1,25,164 square feet including school building, dining hall, library, separate hostel building for higher secondary etc. There is provision of schooling and coaching by subject-wise skilled teachers and madams. There are well-equipped labs to support the study of Chemistry, Physics, Biology, Geography.
